

Main Mechanic:
	Crawling the dungeon:
		
	Inside of a level:
		Player will be taking turns with the enemies inside each level.
		Whoever attacks first will depend on the sneak level of the player??
		
		After the player uses up their amount of turns the enemy will take their turn.
		Each enemy has a set amount of mana that they can use up per turn. 

Level:
	As the player traverses the catacombs(dungeon), they will be pulled into different engagements
	with the enemy. Each one can be considered a "level". Inside of the level there will be 
	random enemies with random cards.
